Further price increases ruled out

PETALING JAYA: Maybank Investment Bank Research (Maybank IB Research) says
consumers should consider adopting mindful spending habits as weak sentiment
could prevail throughout 2024.It said consumers could be forced to prioritise
essential spending over discretionary ones, given elevated staple food and beverage
(F&B) costs and rising household expenditure.“Staple food prices are expected to remain
elevated in 2024 and we do not discount further price increases as operating challenges
persist, in the form of higher labour and utility costs, and raw material price fluctuation
for the domestic food manufacturers,” the research house said in a report.
